Estimation

ASP Level 1 Construction Estimation

iEngineering Australia provides accurate and reliable ASP Level 1 construction estimation services to help clients plan their projects effectively. Recognizing that Level 3 design costs are often significantly lower compared to Level 1 construction costs, we offer estimation services at the concept stage to assist in determining project budget early on.

  • Preliminary Cost Analysis: Providing detailed estimates during the concept stage to establish a realistic project budget.
  • Budget Optimization: Identifying cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality or compliance.
  • Fixed Price Arrangements: Once the Level 3 design is certified by the DNSP, we can arrange a final fixed price for Level 1 construction work for most projects.

Our team’s expertise ensures that you have a clear understanding of project costs from inception to completion.

Survey & 3D Modelling

Surveying and 3D Modelling

Our services begin with advanced powerline surveys, utilizing cutting-edge photogrammetry and LiDAR technology. These techniques deliver precise and reliable data, forming the foundation for effective planning and execution.

The surveyed data can be seamlessly integrated into 3D modelling processes to ensure accuracy and efficiency. 3D digital modelling builds on this surveyed data to create detailed, three-dimensional representations of existing conditions, assets, and proposed protective solutions.

This enables accurate simulation and analysis of various scenarios, allowing for early identification of potential conflicts, optimized design solutions, and compliance with safety standards.

This combined approach enhances construction planning, mitigates risks of asset damage, and ensures protective measures are both practical and effective.
